# Sign up or Join a Class (Individuals or Students)

Go to [https://motiongen.io](https://motiongen.io).

## Sign In

If you have an account, then use your email and password to sign in.

## Sign Up

If you have never used MotionGen before, then sign up. You will need to provide a valid email address, which you have to confirm from your inbox. Thereafter, sign in.

## Join Your Class (Optional)

If you do not have an email address, then you can join a class created by your teacher. You will need your unique username and class code to join which should be provided by your teacher.
